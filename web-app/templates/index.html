About Our Project

It is an advanced AI-based interview chatbot system designed to help students prepare for campus placements and technical interviews. The system provides a fully interactive platform where users can choose to engage in either voice-to-voice or text-to-text mock interview sessions with an AI-powered bot. Upon logging in through Google or email, users are prompted to provide their personal and academic information, including an optional resume upload. The chatbot then conducts a realistic interview session based on the selected engineering branch, asking domain-specific technical questions and general HR questions, including self-introductions and behavioral prompts. The chatbot evaluates responses in real-time, provides feedback, and generates a transcript of the session along with a performance grade. With a database trained on 100+ curated questions per domain, IntervuAI simulates real-world interview environments to enhance student confidence and readiness.
